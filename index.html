FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've answered the most common ones below. Can't find yours? Message us directly on WhatsApp or email.

Is crypto futures trading safe?
+
Futures trading carries significant risk — you can lose money. We manage this with strict risk protocols: max 2% risk per trade, stop-losses on every position, and we never use excessive leverage. We're transparent about our results, including losing trades.
How do you manage my funds without access to my withdrawal?
+
We use API keys from your exchange (Binance, Bybit, or OKX) that allow us to place trades only — not withdraw funds. Your exchange password and 2FA stay with you. Your capital never leaves your account.
What is the minimum investment to start?
+
The minimum for our fund management service is $500 USDT. For mentorship plans, it starts at $99/month. We accept USDT transfers, and Nigerian clients can fund via Naira through our local payment partners.
How do I receive my profits?
+
Profits accumulate directly in your exchange account in real time. We do a monthly settlement review, and you can withdraw your profits or reinvest them. It's your account — you're always in control.
Can I invest from outside Nigeria?
+
Absolutely. We work with clients across the UK, USA, Canada, UAE, Ghana, and beyond. Everything is done digitally, and we accept USDT so there are no currency barriers.
Do you guarantee profits?
+
No — and anyone who guarantees profits in trading is lying to you. What we guarantee is disciplined risk management, full transparency, and our best effort on every trade. Our historical results speak for themselves.
How do the token signals work?
+
When our analysts identify a good entry opportunity on a token, we send you a signal via WhatsApp/Telegram with: the token name, entry price range, take-profit targets (TP1, TP2, TP3), and stop-loss level. You then decide whether to act on it.
